WELLFED AT A GLANCE

The vision remains stamping hunger out of Africa, with specific focus on Children, and their mothers. As much as we will be open to donations and support from individuals and organizations, we do not want to operate as an entity that solely depends on donations and grants alone to achieve this vision. We want to lead by example. If anyone/organization will donate their hard earned money towards the furtherance of the vision, we want to be able to demonstrate that we are doing exactly the same thing. Therefore, we seek to build a business round this vision, an “in-house” sponsor of the vision of some sort. Albeit, a business that is not completely detached from the overall vision.
